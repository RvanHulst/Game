Soldiers Concept by 0laffson

#armor #armour #fantasy #fighter #helm #helmet #human #medieval #middleages #pike #renaissance #sitting #soldier #soldiers #standing #sword #uniform #warrior #conceptart #nobackground #zweihander #platearmor (show more)
More concept art of my fantasy world, this time a group of soldiers of Deremor, one of the many kingdoms where the story (that I'll probably never manage to draw/write) takes place. 

I needed some practice about drawing armors, especially now that I finally understood quite well how these ...things work. They are indeed a mess to draw, plates and leather straps everywhere! XD  The guy on the left is probably a royal guard, or a very important person at least, while the other two are more "common" soldiers.

Any comment, suggestion or critique is more than welcome ^^
